Price Range

It's good to have a wide range of prices. However, within the range that's appropriate for your dining establishment, there should be a reasonable range in price. plate size can help you create a wider range of prices.

Cleanliness

The menu itself should be presentable and clean at all times. It's very unappetizing to look at a menu that has smudges, smears, or dirty spots. Try to clean your menus after every customer. This also aids in overall sanitation of your restaurant.

Clarity

Perhaps one of the most important aspects of menu design is clarity. their experience starts to diminish rapidly. Nobody wants to have to pull out their reading glasses or squint under mood lighting to read your menu. Make sure it's professional, but legible.
